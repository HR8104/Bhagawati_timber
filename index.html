Company Profile

Bhagawati Timber's was founded in 1992 and over the years has grown into a trusted manufacturer and supplier of wood-based packaging solutions. We specialize in a wide range of wooden products — including wooden crates, 2-way & 4-way wooden pallets, and wooden boxes — all designed to serve diverse industrial, export and logistics needs. We cater both to domestic and international clients with scalable production capacity and bespoke solutions. Over the years, our operations have expanded from a modest beginning into a multi-branch business, with dedicated staff, reliable manufacturing infrastructure, and a commitment to continuous growth.
